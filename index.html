What I think about AI

There are a couple things that I have to say about AI. All of these things are reasons that I want to work in AI, and I will write about them in that context

  1. The concept of AGI
    • AGI is an acronym for "Artificial General Intelligence". The difference between AGI and ANI (Artificial Narrow Intelligence) is that AGI can do anything at least as well as a human can
  2. AI can help solve big problems
    • Hunger - allocation of food
    • Poverty - allocation of wealth
    • Global Warming - best sustainable practices
    • Expansion - expanding into the cosmos
  3. It is going to be the topic of the future
    • It will replace millions of jobs
    • It will play a role in law, military, the government, and the private sector
    • It may be the last technology created by man
  4. It can be used to create human flourishing
    • Anybody can have anything they want
    • The AI can be used to cultivate human flourishing
    • Humans will have ultimate freedom
